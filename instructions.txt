Welcome to Swaggy Squirrel!!!
You get to dress up the squirrel.

RULES:
You can select one article of clothing from each column.
Once an item is selected from each column, the squirrel will judge the outfit.

Points are based on how much the squirrel likes the pieces. 

Try your best to impress the squirrel.
Press 0 to continue...

Button functions:
-RESTART will allow you to restart 
without getting judged by the squirrel
